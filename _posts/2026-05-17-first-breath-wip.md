I wanted to make a little browser-native diffusion "story game" where matter would spread, get absorbed, and eventually interact with walls, painting, and flow.

The first page of that idea is still here as [First Breath]({{ '/projects/diffusion-first-breath/' | relative_url }}), and it does work as a WebGPU toy. It emits a plume, diffuses it across the field, and renders the whole thing on the GPU.

What never happened was the part after that.

I had ambitions for this to become a sequence of pages with more interaction and more of an actual game structure, but this one stopped at the MVP stage. So this is less of a launch announcement and more of a marker for a side project that almost became something bigger.

There is still a thing there if you want to click around. Just do not mistake "there is a working page" for "this turned into a finished project," because it definitely did not.
