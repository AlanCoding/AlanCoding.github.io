🐘❄️ Petawatt Radiators and Beyond

Kardashev range: 1.2–2.7+

Once industrial activity gets large enough, waste heat dominates everything. Radiator architecture becomes a first-order design constraint, from mobile cold ships all the way up to immense thermal infrastructure.

Visuals

These renders start to frame the scale and layout problem. I will expand this page later with the actual argument, edge cases, and variants.

Notes

The deeper question is how heat rejection scales once available power outruns compact radiators by absurd margins, and what kinds of structures appear before true stellar-scale engineering.
